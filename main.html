What is Self Rate?

Self Rate is a website with which you can better understand how the IELTS exam works, as well as find out your English reading and listening level, and get guidance and preparation materials.

Testing system

IELTS Reading and Listening test system, which is close to the real IELTS exam

Level analysis

Assessing your level according to IELTS rules (band 1 - 9) and highlighting your strengths and weaknesses

Material Library

A library specially selected for successful preparation for the Reading and Listening parts of the program
